Mommas Mac N' Cheese

Ingredients

  • Kraft Mac N' Cheese Box with noodles inside
  • 6 Cups of Diamond Filtered Water
  • 4 Table Spoons of Pure Irish Cow Butter
  • 1/4 Cups of Milk

Directions

  1. Bring all 6 cups of water to a rolling boil
  2. Add noodles to boiling water and cook fo EXACTLY 7 1/2 minutes
  3. Strain noodles into sink
  4. Glob the ungodly amount of butter along with the milk and the cheese into the pot. Stir until creamy
  5. Add noodles back into pot and stir until this sound is emitting
